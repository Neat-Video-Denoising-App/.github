## 🚀 Introduction
Neat Video for Windows is a purpose-built denoising plugin that lives inside your editing software and takes on the noise and grain that show up in footage shot in low light or at high ISO. Because it runs as a plugin in hosts such as Premiere Pro, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve, you clean your clips on the same timeline you cut and grade on. It measures the actual noise in a shot, constructs a profile from it, and reduces that noise while working to preserve real image detail. For editors, colorists, and compositors, that means cleaner footage without adding a separate step to the pipeline.

## ✨ Highlights

### Clean, profile-driven results
- **Noise profiling** — Sample a frame so the reduction is based on the true noise in your footage.
- **Detail preservation** — Reduce noise and grain while trying to hold on to fine texture and sharpness.
- **Temporal filtering** — Smooth noise across neighboring frames for steadier, cleaner motion.

### A workflow that fits your editor
- **In-editor plugin** — Apply Neat Video as an effect without leaving your host application.
- **Live before/after preview** — Compare original and processed frames inside the neat video plugin interface as you tune.
- **Pro edition scaling** — The Neat Video Pro tier targets heavier projects with higher performance and more simultaneous streams.

## 🎯 Getting Up and Running
1. Add a noisy clip to your timeline and apply the Neat Video effect to it.
2. Open the plugin and build a noise profile automatically, or select a flat, detail-free region to profile manually.
3. Switch to the before/after preview to see how much noise is removed.
4. Adjust reduction and sharpening controls for a clean but natural look.
5. Return to your timeline and render or export as usual.

## ❓ Common Questions
**Is Neat Video free?**
Neat Video is a paid plugin. A demo/trial is offered, so your first neat video download can be used to test the workflow and results in your own project before you buy a license.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
Neat Video for Windows runs on current 64-bit versions of Windows, including Windows 10 and Windows 11, alongside a supported host editing application.

**What about neat video vs topaz?**
Both are respected options serving different needs. Neat Video specializes in noise and grain reduction as a plugin inside your editor, while Topaz offers AI-based enhancement tools across a broader range. The best choice comes down to whether you need dedicated denoising in your timeline or wider AI enhancement.

**Is my data secure?**
Neat Video processes your footage locally inside your host editor on your own machine, so your media stays under your control throughout.
